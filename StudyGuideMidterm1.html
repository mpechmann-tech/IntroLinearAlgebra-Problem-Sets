Problem 1

In each part, determine whether the equation is linear and whether the equation is homogeneous.

  1. (i)

    x⁢y=1

  2. (ii)

    x1+5⁢x2−2⁢x3=1

  3. (iii)

    y+7=x

  4. (iv)

    x1=−7⁢x2+3⁢x3

  5. (v)

    x1+3⁢x2+x1⁢x2=2

  6. (vi)

    sin⁡(2)⁢x1+π⁢x2−2=0
